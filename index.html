How the Credit Works

What is Panda credit?

Panda offers eligible customers access to a £300 MCC-locked virtual Mastercard for use exclusively at hospitality venues — restaurants, bars, cafes, pubs and similar establishments. The credit is provided by SteadyPay Limited and repaid over three months.

How is the credit used?

Once approved, your £300 credit is loaded onto a virtual Mastercard issued via Equals Money. The card is locked to hospitality merchant category codes (MCCs) and cannot be used for any other category of spend. This ensures the credit is used only for its intended purpose.

Repayment Schedule

  • Payment 1: £112 (£100 principal + £12 fee share) — due 30 days after funds received
  • Payment 2: £112 — due 60 days after funds received
  • Payment 3: £112 — due 90 days after funds received

Repayments are collected by SteadyPay Limited via direct debit. You will be required to set up a direct debit mandate during the application process.

Fees

Panda charges a flat one-off fee of £36 (12% of the £300 credit) per credit cycle. There are no interest charges, no rollover fees, and no hidden costs. The fee is payable alongside your repayments as set out above.

Late or Missed Payments

If you miss a repayment, SteadyPay Limited may report this to credit reference agencies, which may negatively affect your credit score. If you are struggling to make repayments, please contact SteadyPay directly at support@steadypay.co as soon as possible.

Your Right to Withdraw

You have the right to withdraw from your credit agreement within 14 days of entering into it, without giving any reason. To exercise this right, contact SteadyPay Limited at support@steadypay.co. You will be required to repay the credit in full within 30 days of giving notice of withdrawal.

Credit Checks & Affordability

All credit is subject to status. SteadyPay Limited will carry out identity verification, credit reference checks, and affordability assessments before approving your application. Panda does not make credit decisions — all decisions are made by SteadyPay Limited.
